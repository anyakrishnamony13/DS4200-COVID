How Transportation Mode Use Shifted Before, During, and After COVID

Before and During show what share of respondents used each mode at each frequency tier. Expected After shows whether respondents expect their use to increase or decrease relative to before COVID.

⚠️ Note: The "Expected After" view uses a different scale than Before/During. Bars here show expected change relative to before COVID (e.g. "Much less" to "Much more"), not absolute usage frequency. Direct visual comparison with the other two periods should be made cautiously.
